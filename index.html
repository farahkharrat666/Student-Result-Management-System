About the University

The ENET'Com is an institution of higher education and scientific research with the mission the training of engineers, license, Masters and PhD. It was created in 2002 under the name ISECS (Higher Institute of Electronics and Communication of Sfax) on July 9, 2002 for the training of Senior Technicians. Then by decree n ° 2014-105 of January 16, 2014, it changed its name to become the National School of Electronics and Telecommunications of Sfax (ENET'Com).

BACHELOR

The unified license in Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) meets the needs of modern society. Mastery of information and communication technologies is the key component of this training. It also makes it possible to respond to the trends currently prevailing at the national and international level. The training gives students the basic knowledge on subjects and tools: mathematics, telecommunications, electronics, conceptual, networks, computer science, etc. necessary for the development of applications, databases, etc.

TELECOMMUNICATIONS ENGINEERING

The "Telecommunications Engineering" field of engineers is part of the objective of meeting the needs of companies in the sectors of information technology and telecommunications (ICT). This branch introduces quality training in new telecommunications technologies and network engineering and administration. This training cycle is spread over four semesters reserved for general academic training, with theoretical, methodological and applied aspects. At the end of the second year, each engineering student admitted to the third year will be assigned to one of the following three options

PROFESSIONAL MASTER

Taking into account the development of the computer and telecommunications fabric on a national and international scale, the professional master's degree in Computer Networks and Telecommunications (RITEL) is in line with the objective of meeting the needs of companies in terms of engineering networks. on the one hand and management of information systems on the web on the other.

DOCTORATE

The doctoral degree in Information and Communication Science and Technologies (ICST) offered by the National School of Electronics and Telecommunications of Sfax (ENET'Com) concerns doctoral studies in ICST covering various but complementary themes. : Telecommunications Networks, Security, Digital Communications Systems, Radiocommunication, Telecommunications Systems Architecture, Electronics, Embedded Systems, Business Intelligence, Artificial Intelligence,…
